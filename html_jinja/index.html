Mission

The TigerClimb app seeks to improve the overall experience of students who participate in the Outdoor Action climbing wall, are part of the climbing team, and anyone else who climbs the wall. The climbing wall is located in Power Field Stadium and visited by hundreds of students every week. Our motivation is to increase the accessibility to new climbers and provide new information to all climbers. New to climbing? Reference Beni's Big Ol' List of Climbing Terms, by Beni Snow '19

Our Motive

Motivation

The Princeton Outdoor Action climbing wall is visited by hundreds of students each semester. The wall is made of a grid of holes, into which “holds” are dynamically bolted to create set paths (“routes”). Therein, however, lies an issue: you climb following a set route, but the routes are poorly documented, creating a barrier of access to the wall. Our product will create an accessible way to learn more about the wall, find routes, and find route information. At its core, our product will provide detailed information about each route and a way to search for and sort routes, and our front page will allow people to visualize the routes and explore them in an intuitive manner.
